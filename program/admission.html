Deadline and Requirements

List of Documents required for registration IAAU
  1. Original High School Diploma of Graduation (including high school transcript).
  2. Original “ORT” certificate.
  3. Copy of ID Card.
  4. Copy of Military Registration Certificate (boys only)
  5. ID Photo: size 3x4 – (8 pieces).
  6. Medical Certificate № 086.
  7. Initial Payment of 30% from total Tuition Fee.

Who eligible for Financial Aid?

  • Applicants, who took the 1st place in Republican Olympiads in the following subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Computer, Kyrgyz, Turkish and Russian - study for free. Those who took the 2nd place will receive 50% of discount and the 3rd place - 40%. Medalists of Olympiads are free in choice of departments to study.

  • For Applicants graduating next academic year, who took the 1st place in national Olympiads in the following subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Computer, Kyrgyz, Turkish and Russian the discount is 25%. Medalists of Olympiads are free in choice of departments.

  • Applicants who took 1st place at the International Olympiad on the Turkish language, the discount are %100, the 2nd place - 50%, 3rd place - 40%.

  • General Republican Test (GRT)1 Certificate holders within top 100 results get 100% discount in any department.

    • Each below discount is given only one quota on our department.

    • GRT Certificate holders within top 200 results get 50% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 300 results get 40% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 500 results get 30% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 1000 results get 25% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 2000 results get 20% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 3000 results get 15% discount.
    • GRT Certificate holders within top 5000 results get 10% discount.
  • Applicants with a “Red” High School Graduation Diplomas get a discount of 15%.
  • Applicants, who took the 1st place in regional Olympiad, get a discount of 20%.
  • Students from one family have a right on common discount of %10.
Scholarships

Starting from second year in case of success, three students with top three GPA, from each department awarded scholarships in amount of:

First Place - 2000 soms

Second place - 1500 soms

Third place - 1000 soms

Note

1. Scholarships are granted for one semester only.

2. Beside the scholarship for social activity, student can take either discount or another scholarship. For example, students who studied with a discount of %100 or %50 cannot take the scholarship.

Important

All of the above information is valid during 2013-2014 Academic Years and is maybe different next Academic Year.
